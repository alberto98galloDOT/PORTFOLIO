Fire Risk · Weighted Overlay

Blue Mountains Bushfire Risk Assessment

Burn Severity · dNBR

Southern Western Downs Burn Severity

Queensland, Australia

Differenced Normalised Burn Ratio (dNBR) derived from Landsat 9 OLI-2. Five severity classes calibrated against USGS/FIREMON thresholds. Intersected with QLUMP 2019 land use for impact assessment.

Habitat Mapping · Gap Analysis

Wet Tropics Habitat Mapping & Conservation Gap Analysis

Cairns Regional LGA, Queensland, Australia

Essential Habitat mapping under VMA 1999. Southern Cassowary as indicator species (n=372 records). Gap analysis reveals 8.7% of Essential Habitat formally protected — 74.5% of cassowary records outside protected areas.

Coastal Risk · Bathtub Model

Port Adelaide Coastal Inundation Assessment

Gulf St Vincent, South Australia

Bathtub inundation model with hydraulic connectivity correction (GRASS r.clump) on 1m LiDAR. Sea-level-rise scenario +1.0m (2100) floods 36.8 ha and exposes 58 buildings at Birkenhead. Planning exposure mapped against the SA Planning & Design Code.
